Living in Mumbai — what you need to know

Mumbai is India financial capital and most expensive city. Salaries in finance, tech, and consulting can be competitive but housing in prime areas is increasingly expensive.

Tax situation

Income tax up to 30% for high earners. LTCG tax of 10% on equity gains above 1 lakh INR. Section 80C allows 1.5L deduction.

Investing in Mumbai

Zerodha or Groww for direct mutual fund investing. Nifty 50 index funds are the standard FIRE vehicle.
