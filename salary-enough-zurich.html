Living in Zurich — what you need to know

Zurich has the highest cost of living of any city in this list — but also among the highest salaries. Finance, pharma, and tech sectors pay exceptionally well. Net savings potential is high despite costs.

Tax situation

Swiss income tax varies by canton. Zurich effective rate for middle earners is roughly 25-30%. Wealth tax applies to net assets.

Investing in Zurich

Pillar 3a pension contributions are tax-deductible. Interactive Brokers or Swissquote for investing.
